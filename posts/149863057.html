原子弹的爆炸原理和制作流程

July 11, 2023 Physics, Atomic Bomb 本文总阅读量次

在此录入一些我对原子弹的理解，由基础到深入

首先你需要原料（铀矿），然后你需要把铀矿里的铀元素精炼出来

然后你要把这个精炼好的，纯度非常高的铀元素做成一个球的样子，因为这样能够最大限度，最有可能的让它充分的链式反应

然后你需要在这个球的外圈非常均匀的装一些高强度的化学炸药，这样炸药引爆以后可以最大限度的给中心的铀球施加足够致使其产生链式反应的压力和热量（这两个的区别我不太分得清楚，反正就那么回事

然后原子弹就爆炸了，这就是原子弹的爆炸原理。

推荐书籍

The Los Alamos primer

an amazing document this is the lectures that were given to the scientists as they were arriving at Los Alamos for the Manhattan Project in about 1943 with lots of additional notes and information by the guy who gave the lectures it really gives you a good introduction to the physics of nuclear weapons as it was understood in 1943 with some updates in the notes.

the making of the atomic bomb by Richard Rhodes(page tuner introduction)

if you want a wonderful page-turner historical account of how nuclear weapons were created in the Manhattan Project the making of the atomic bomb by Richard Rhodes is really a classic it's it's I think understandable by everyone and yet it gets the physics more or less right if you want a more technical account of the Manhattan Project.

critical assembly by hottes and Henriksen Mead and Westfall(more technical)

is much drier than the making of the atomic bomb but much more detailed on the technical front on the effects of nuclear weapons the US government's textbook on that subject by Gladstone and Dolan is called the effects of nuclear weapons and it's still available and in fact it's been put online in PDF form if you search for it

the physical medical and social effects of the atomic bombs

and if you want a detailed account of the impact of the bombings of Hiroshima and Nagasaki there's a book called the physical medical and social effects of the atomic bombings that really is a nice job on The making of atomic bomb.
